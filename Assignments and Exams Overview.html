Assignments and Exams Overview

This course will have three assignments and two exams (a midterm exam and a final exam).

Questions on the assignments will include:

  • Programming questions involving math concepts
  • Math questions involving calculations, sometimes done in Excel
  • Concept questions related to math topics

Marks for individual questions will be given on the assignments.

Questions on the exams will include:

  • Programming questions involving math concepts
  • Math questions involving calculations
  • Concept questions related to math topics

Marks for individual questions will be given on the exams. Exams will be handwritten. A formula sheet will be provided.
